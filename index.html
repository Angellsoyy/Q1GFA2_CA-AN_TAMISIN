Street Woman FIghter 2

Street Woman Fighter 2 (Korean: 스트릿 우먼 파이터 2), often abbreviated SWF2, is the third season of the South Korean dance competition franchise Street Woman Fighter. It premiered on Mnet on August 22, 2023, and airs every Tuesday at 22:20 KST.[1] It revolves around eight female dance crews fighting for the position of Korea's best female dance crew to represent street dance in the country (loosely marketed under the term "K-dance").[2] The winning crew will receive ₩50 million and diamond rings from Amondz.
